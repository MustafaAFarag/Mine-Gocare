Privacy and Policy

Information Collected

  • First name, last name, address, area and city, phone and contact numbers, email address, GPS location (mobile site), and ID.
  • Past Go Care reservations, favorite beauty centers and salons, customer service inquiries, and certain social networking preferences.

Analytics and Data Tracking

  • Analytics software is used to track and analyze traffic for advertising and promotion.
  • Statistics may be shared with third parties without including PII.
  • Non-PII includes demographic info, mobile type, screen resolution, OS version, browser, etc.

Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

  • Cookies, pixel tags, and web beacons enhance user experience and tailor information.
  • IP addresses are automatically collected and logged.

Use of Information

  • Used to create accounts, communicate, offer services, promotions, and process payments.
  • Cookies remember users for a better website experience.

Sharing of Information

  • Information is not sold to third parties.
  • PII may be shared with vendors, service providers, beauty centers, or for legal purposes.

User-Generated Content

  • Content posted by users may be visible to the general public; privacy is not guaranteed.

Handling of Non-PII

  • Non-PII may be used for any purpose and shared with affiliates or third parties.

Email Address

  • Required for account registration and reservation communication.
  • Users can update their email through their account.
  • May be shared with beauty centers for reservation purposes.

Payment Information

  • Go Care does not store or collect debit/credit card information.
  • Payment methods include cash, credit card, or payment checks.

Reservations Placement Information

  • Personal data collected during reservations is shared with salons with confidentiality.

Navigation and Click Stream Data

  • Collected for analysis and improving user experience.

Advertising and Marketing

  • Cookies may be used to measure advertising effectiveness.
  • Personal data is not sold or rented without explicit consent.

Disclosure of Personal Information

  • May be disclosed to staff, service providers, or for legal reasons.
  • May be disclosed in events such as mergers or reorganizations.

Cookies

  • Used for identification and can be managed via browser settings.

Protection of Personal Information

  • Secure pages and encryption technology protect data (SSL).

Security Measures

  • Organizational, technical, and administrative protections are in place.
  • No method is 100% secure over the Internet.

Third Parties

  • Not responsible for third-party practices or linked sites.

Information about Services

  • Users may receive details about discounts, promotions, and campaigns.

Policy Amendment

  • The policy may be updated and will be published on the app or site.

Verifying, Updating, and Amending Personal Information

  • Users can update their personal information through their account settings.

Delete Personal Data

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the profile page, then Account Settings.
  3. Click "Delete Account" to remove your data or account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.
  5. We recommend reviewing the policy before deletion.
